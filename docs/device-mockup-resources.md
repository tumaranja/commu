# Device mockup templates (no scratch work)

**Official (truest to current iPhones):** Apple lists **Product Bezels** (e.g. iPhone 17, iPhone 16) on **[Apple Design Resources](https://developer.apple.com/design/resources/)**. Downloads are **Sketch-based** (“Sketch must be installed to download”). Before publishing marketing shots, follow **[Marketing Resources and Identity Guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/app-store/marketing/guidelines/#section-products)**.

**Fast, browser-based (drop a screenshot):** e.g. **[Mediamodifier — iPhone mockup generator](https://mediamodifier.com/c/iphone-mockup-generator)** — pick a template, upload PNG, export (check their license for your use case).

**Figma:** Search Community for **“iPhone 17 Pro mockup”** or **“Dynamic Island”** — many free frames; quality varies.

This prototype’s in-app frame is **CSS-only** (`AppShell` + `index.css`); for **Soon-style** marketing images, use one of the sources above and **replace** static exports, not the live shell.
